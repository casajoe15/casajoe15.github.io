How to Put Your Iphone in Low Power Mode in simple steps!

Welcome to the my tutorial for how to put your current iphone into low power mode to save battery life.

Today you will learn how to do this in simple steps.
Click on the image to begin

  • Turn on your iphone

  • Unlock your iphone

  • From your homescreen, click on the settings application

  • Scroll down till you see the tab labeled battery

  • Click on low power to start saving your battery. And done!
