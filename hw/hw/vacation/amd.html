Beaches

Maui has some of the most beautiful beaches in the world. Some of these beaches include Baldwin Beach, Oneuli Beach, and Keawakapu Beach.

  • Baldwin Beach
  • Keawakapu Beach
  • Oneuli Beach

Activities

There are countless number of activities to do in Maui. From biking through Makawao Forest, mountain hiking on the Kapalua Coastal Trail, surfing, and exploring the volcanos, there is enough on the island to keep anyone occupied.

  • Makawao Forest
  • Kapalua Coastal Trail

Spas

The resorts in Maui have some of the most soothing spas in the entire world. They have many ways to keep you relaxed such as massages, mud baths, cupping theorpy, and saunas. These spas make it feel like you are in heaven.
